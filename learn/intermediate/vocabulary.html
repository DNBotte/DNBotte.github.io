VOCABULARY

For Intermediate Level Learners

How to improve your vocabulary

  • Vocab Book

    The first thing any learner of any level should do is create a vocab book.

  • Read often

    Reading provides the best exposure to new words and the proper useage of them. This is advised by nearly all books that address the need to expand your vocabulary. It is also recommended that you read with your vocab book open, if possible. Combining the two is probably the most effective and efficient way to improve.

    Print material is very useful because you can underline, circle, or highlight new words as you go.

    You can get started on your reading right here

  • Revise your writing thoroughly

    This is a great opportunity to improve your writing and exercise your vocabulary. As you revise, think of better ways to express things. Often this can be done by replacing longer phrases with shorter ones or even a single word. If you are not doing a simulated writing exercise, you can use a thesaurus such as Thesaurus.com. You can also do this while revising your writing after a simulation.
