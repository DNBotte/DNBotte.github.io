Welcome Righi Students!

to Learning English with David Botte

My name is David and I am excited to help you improve your English.

Today English is by far the most studied foreign language in the world. Learning it is just as useful and important as knowing how to use your smartphone or computer. Traveling will be easier. Information will be more abundant. Opportunities will open up. Most importantly, you will understand 90% of the funny memes, Tweets, and videos on the internet.

I believe the best way to learn a language is through interaction in the classroom because I am passionate about the philosophy of "work smarter, not harder". My objective is to develop these skills in each and every one of you, not just so you can pass an exam and hang a certificate on your refrigerator. I want to empower you with one of the most valuable tools of the 21st Century.

You may begin this journey by exploring the online content of this course on an online platform called Moodle: dnbottesolutions.moodlecloud.com

When you are done, be sure to visit the course homepage at davidnbotte.space/learn/Righi17
